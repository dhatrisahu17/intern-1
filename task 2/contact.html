About Me

The person behind the code.

Developer, designer, and accessibility advocate based in New York City. I believe the web works best when it works for everyone.

Biography

Hello, I'm Alex

I'm a full-stack developer and UI/UX designer with over 7 years of experience building web applications that balance beautiful design with rigorous accessibility. I started my career in graphic design before teaching myself to code — a path that left me with an unusual blend of aesthetic sensibility and engineering discipline.

My work is guided by a simple principle: good design is invisible, and accessible design is good design. I've shipped products used by millions of people, and I've learned that no user is "edge case."

When I'm not at a keyboard, I organise local meetups on web accessibility, contribute to open-source tooling, and (occasionally) go for runs.
